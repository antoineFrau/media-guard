## What it is
Flag-waving is a manipulation technique that leverages patriotic or nationalistic symbols, language, and appeals to evoke loyalty and emotional attachment. It often involves invoking a sense of collective identity and unity, using terms that resonate with national pride and patriotism.

## How to recognize it
Flag-waving can be recognized by the use of patriotic language, symbols, or appeals that evoke emotions related to national identity. Common trigger terms include:
- "We the People" (EN), "Nous le peuple" (FR)
- "Patriotism" (EN), "Patriotisme" (FR)
- "National pride" (EN), "Fierté nationale" (FR)
- "Country" (EN), "Pays" (FR)
- "Freedom" (EN), "Liberté" (FR)
- "Justice" (EN), "Justice" (FR)

This technique often appears in political discourse, media, and social commentary to rally support, dismiss opposing views, or justify actions under the guise of national interest.

## Examples
- "Mueller attempts to stop the will of We the People!!! It's time to jail Mueller."
- "Nous devons protéger notre pays contre les menaces étrangères pour préserver notre liberté et notre justice."
