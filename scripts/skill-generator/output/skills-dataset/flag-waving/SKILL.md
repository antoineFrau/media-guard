## What it is
Flag-waving is a manipulation technique that leverages patriotic or nationalistic symbols, language, and appeals to evoke loyalty, emotional attachment, and a sense of belonging. It is often used to rally support, frame opposition as unpatriotic, or evoke loyalty. This technique can be found in political discourse, news articles, and social media, where it is used to manipulate public opinion by appealing to national identity and in-group pride.

## How to recognize it
Flag-waving can be recognized by the use of patriotic or nationalistic language, symbols, and appeals. Common trigger terms in English include "patriots," "real Americans," "American strength," and "support our troops." In French, terms like "drapeau," "patriote," and "fierté nationale" may be used. The technique often involves slogans and appeals to in-group pride. According to PropaInsight (COLING 2025), the appeals evoked include patriotism, national identity, and belonging, with the intent to evoke loyalty, rally support, or frame opposition as unpatriotic.

## Examples
- "Mueller attempts to stop the will of We the People!!! It's time to jail Mueller."
- "True patriots stand with our troops. Real Americans support our borders."
- "We need to show the world American strength. Make America great again."
- "Stand with our veterans. Support our flag. America deserves leaders who love this country."
