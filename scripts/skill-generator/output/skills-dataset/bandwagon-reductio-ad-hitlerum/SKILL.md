## What it is
Bandwagon is a propaganda technique that suggests widespread support for a position, implying the audience should conform. Reductio ad Hitlerum is a logical fallacy that discredits an argument by associating it with Hitler or Nazism, often using loaded terms like "Vichy journalism" to evoke collaboration with Nazis.

## How to recognize it
- **Bandwagon**: Phrases like "everyone agrees," "join the bandwagon," or "don't be left out."
- **Reductio ad Hitlerum**: Comparisons to Hitler, Nazism, or terms like "Vichy collaborator" to discredit an argument.
- **Context**: Often used in political discourse or media to manipulate opinions by appealing to popularity or fear.

## Examples
- "Everyone is supporting this policy, so you should too." (Bandwagon)
- ""Vichy journalism," a term which now fits so much of the mainstream media. It collaborates in the same way that the Vichy government in France collaborated with the Nazis." (Reductio ad Hitlerum)
