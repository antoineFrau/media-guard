## What it is
Name Calling/Labeling is a manipulation technique that involves using negative labels or epithets to describe a person or group. The purpose is to discredit the target by attaching pejorative terms, often to influence public opinion or sway political discourse.

## How to recognize it
1. **Negative Labels**: Look for pejorative terms or negative labels applied to individuals or groups.
2. **Contextual Clues**: The use of such terms often occurs in political or media discourse to discredit opponents.
3. **Emotional Tone**: The language used is typically emotionally charged and intended to provoke a negative reaction.

## Examples
- "WHO: Coronavirus emergency is 'Public Enemy Number 1'"
- "The radical left is destroying our country."
- "He's nothing but a crook and a liar."
