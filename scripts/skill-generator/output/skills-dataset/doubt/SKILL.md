## What it is
Doubt is a manipulation technique where the credibility of a person, institution, or claim is questioned or undermined. This is often done to create skepticism and erode trust in the target.

## How to recognize it
Doubt can be recognized by phrases that imply uncertainty or lack of confidence. Common trigger terms in English include "Can the same be said for...?", "Is it really true that...?", and "Are we sure about...?". In French, similar phrases include "Peut-on en dire autant de...?", "Est-ce vraiment vrai que...?", and "En est-on vraiment sûr...?".

## Examples
- "Can the same be said for the Obama Administration?"
- "Is it really true that the government is handling the crisis effectively?"
- "Peut-on en dire autant de l'administration Obama?"
- "Est-ce vraiment vrai que le gouvernement gère bien la crise?"
