## What it is
Détournement de la réalité is a manipulation technique that involves presenting a distorted or misleading version of reality to influence public opinion or justify a position. This technique can be used to downplay or exaggerate certain aspects of reality, or to present a completely false version of events. It is often used in political and media contexts to shape public perception and support a particular agenda.

## How to recognize it
To recognize Détournement de la réalité, look for the following indicators:
- False or misleading claims about the existence or nature of social or political phenomena.
- Exaggeration or downplaying of certain aspects of reality.
- Use of loaded language or emotive terms to shape perception.
- Lack of evidence or credible sources to support claims.
- Contradictions or inconsistencies in the presentation of information.
