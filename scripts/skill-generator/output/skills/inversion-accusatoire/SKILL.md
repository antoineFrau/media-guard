# Inversion Accusatoire

## What It Is
Inversion accusatoire is a rhetorical manipulation technique where an individual or group accuses their adversary of actions or intentions that they themselves possess. This technique is often used to divert attention from one's own actions, discredit opponents, or invert roles to create confusion or shift blame.

## How to Recognize It
1. **Role Inversion**: Identify statements where the accuser attributes actions or intentions to the opponent that are actually characteristic of the accuser.
2. **Contextual Inconsistency**: Look for inconsistencies between the accusation and the known facts or behaviors of the parties involved.
3. **Emotional Language**: Accusations are often framed with strong emotional language to evoke a reaction and sway public opinion.
4. **Deflection**: The technique is frequently used to deflect attention from the accuser's own actions or shortcomings.

- **"Les antifascistes sont bel et bien les fascistes d'aujourd'hui."** (video: Iizvn8ZGma4) - Accusation des antifascistes d'être fascistes, inversant ainsi les rôles.
- **"La France insoumise a fait de la jeune garde son bras armé. pour semer la terreur et la violence à l'université ou dans la rue."** (video: Iizvn8ZGma4) - Accusation de la France insoumise de semer la terreur, alors que les faits montrent une violence principalement venue de l'extrême droite.
