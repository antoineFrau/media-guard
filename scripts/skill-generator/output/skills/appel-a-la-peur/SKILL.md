## What it is
"Appel à la peur" is a rhetorical manipulation technique that leverages fear to sway public opinion or behavior. It involves using dramatic language, catastrophic scenarios, or implicit threats to create a sense of urgency or danger. This technique is often employed in political and media contexts to influence perceptions and actions.

## How to recognize it
1. **Dramatic Language**: Look for exaggerated or sensational language that evokes strong emotional responses.
2. **Catastrophic Scenarios**: Identify scenarios that depict extreme, negative outcomes if certain actions are not taken.
3. **Implicit Threats**: Notice subtle or indirect threats that suggest harm or danger if the audience does not comply with the desired behavior or opinion.
4. **Urgency**: Pay attention to phrases that create a sense of immediate action or consequence.
