## What it is
"Dénigrement de l'adversaire" is a rhetorical manipulation technique that involves discrediting or denigrating an opponent to weaken their position or argumentation. This can include personal attacks, negative generalizations, or other forms of disparagement aimed at undermining an adversary's credibility or message.

## How to recognize it
1. **Personal Attacks**: Look for statements that attack the character, motives, or integrity of the opponent rather than addressing their arguments.
2. **Negative Generalizations**: Identify broad, negative statements about a group or individual that are not substantiated by evidence.
3. **Disparagement**: Notice any attempts to belittle, mock, or ridicule the opponent or their ideas.
4. **Contextual Clues**: Pay attention to the tone and language used, which may be overly critical, dismissive, or hostile.
